Below is a list of everything you’ll need to make an easy and delicious cinnamon cream cheese churro made from bread and a few other ingredients!

✔ Ingredients

  • 1 Loaf white bread
  • 1 8 oz. package cream cheese (softened)
  • ¾ cup confectioners (powdered) sugar
  • ½ stick margarine (melted)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon

✔ Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Line large cookie sheet with parchment paper
  3. Remove crust from bread
  4. Flatten bread with a rolling pin
  5. Combine cream cheese and confectioners sugar in a bowl
  6. Combine sugar and cinnamon in a separate bowl, set aside
  7. Spread ~1 tbsp. of cream cheese mixture on each slice of bread
  8. Roll each slice of bread
  9. Brush with melted margarine
  10. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar mix
  11. Place on cookie sheet (with parchment paper)
  12. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 20 minutes or until golden brown

Note: Cream cheese mixture can be substituted with peanut butter or filling of your choice.
