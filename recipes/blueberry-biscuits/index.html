Below is a list of everything you’ll need to make delicious blueberry biscuits with a sweet glaze to compliment them nicely.

✔ Ingredients

  • Biscuits
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 4 tbsp. margarine
  • 1 tbsp. margarine (melted)
  • 4 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 to 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • Glaze
  • 1 1/2 cups confectioners (powdered) sugar
  • 1/8 cup water
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp. lemon juice

✔ Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Hand mix the flour, sugar, 4 tbsp. margarine, baking powder, and salt
  3. Slowly add milk until soft dough texture
  4. Add blueberries to mixture
  5. Flatten dough onto floured board and cut biscuits to shape
  6. Place onto ungreased baking sheet
  7. Bake for 7-12 minute or until golden brown
  8. Remove from oven and brush with 1 tbsp. melted margarine
  9. Cool slightly and drizzle with glaze
