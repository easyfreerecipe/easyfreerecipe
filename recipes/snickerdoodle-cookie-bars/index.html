Here’s a quick and easy way to make some soft moist snickerdoodle cookies when you don’t have a lot of time.

✔ Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp. cinnamon

✔ Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. In a large bowl, add the margarine and sugar and mix
  3. Add the eggs and mix
  4. Add sour cream and mix by hand
  5. Add baking soda, cream of tartar and salt and mix by hand
  6. Add the flour and mix well
  7. In a small bowl, combine the sugar and cinnamon
  8. Sprinkle half the cinnamon mixture into a 9×13 in. pan
  9. Spread cookie mix into pan
  10. Top with other half of cinnamon mixture
  11. Bake 350° for 25 to 30 minutes.
