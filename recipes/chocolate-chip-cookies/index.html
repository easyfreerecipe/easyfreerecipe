Below is a list of everything you’ll need to make your chocolate chip cookies and directions to be eating them in 20 minutes!

✔ Ingredients

  • 1 stick margarine
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar (lightly packed)
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ cup flour
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

✔ Directions

  1. Preheat oven 350° F (175° C)
  2. Combine first four ingredients & beat with mixer until smooth
  3. Add baking soda and vanilla extract, then stir
  4. Add flour, stirring until mixed
  5. Add chips
  6. Drop large spoonfuls on lightly greased cookie sheet
  7. Place in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until edges lightly browned
  8. Allow cookies to cool for 5 minutes on baking sheet, then completely on wire rack
